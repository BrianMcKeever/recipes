Ingredients:
1 8 oz package cream cheese
pie shell (graham or plain)
15 oz can sweetened condensed milk
1/3 cup lemon juice
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
6 oz can of cherry pie filling

Directions:
1) Bake shell as directed.
2) Let cream cheese soften at room temperature.
3) Beat cheese until fluffy.
4) Gradually add condensed milk while beating until well blended.
5) Add lemon juice and vanilla extract, blend well.
6) Pour into baked shell.
7) Chill for 2 to 3 hours
8) Garnish with cherry pie filling.
